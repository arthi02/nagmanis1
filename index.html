Frequently Asked Questions

  • How to reach customer if there is any damage in product ?

    Please send photo proof of damaged product within 24hrs to customer or email the proof info@nagmanis.com

  • How do I register my business in Shopilo.ca if I choose selling option?

    step 1 - Please click sell local

    step 2 - Then Click become a vendor in register page,

    step 3 - Choose any one of the subscription plan,

    step 4 - Do the registration process, After registration you have to wait 1 business day to activate the account . If you any doubts feel free to email us info@nagmanis.com we are here to support local business.

  • How do I register as a customer in Shopilo.ca if I want to shop ?

    step 1 - Select the product you want to buy

    step 2 - Go to cart

    step 3 - Billing & shipping section you have to enter your credentials

    step 4 - Payment page Finally your account will be created from next time if you want to login as customer use your previous credentials to login . If you have any doubts feel free to email us info@nagmanis.com we are here to support our customers to shop locally online.
